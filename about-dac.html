About DAC

Food is necessary, but cooking is an art. And when you’re the one cooking, there is always room for a little experimentation. Desi Atta Company is about the hundreds of dishes we discovered, and the infinite masterpieces you bring to life in your kitchen every day. With over 30 different types of flour variants and ready mixes, we aim to reduce the effort you need to put in to enjoy your favorite flour based delicacies, and make cooking not just a chore but a fun, experimental process. And not just that, Desi Atta Company’s range includes products you can easily integrate into your every meal. One thing we have learnt is that you, the new Desi, are constantly reinventing yourself and the world. Whether you’re up at 4 am or till 4 am, whether you struggle to deal with the boss at office or on your Playstation, whether you earn success the traditional way or have the coolest jugaad, your situations are constantly changing. No matter who you are, at Desi Atta Company we have something for every situation. Don’t just cook, start experimenting!
Desi Atta Company is a brand by Future Consumer Limited. We believe in creating products that promote a better lifestyle for our consumers.

At Desi Atta Company, our primary aim is to offer convenient products to our customers while never compromising on quality. We ensure that only the finest ingredients are used in making our products so as to offer the tastiest and healthiest products to our consumers. We are firmly committed to being a catalyst of positive change in the life of every family member.
