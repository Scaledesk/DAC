Ragi Atta Halwa

Ingredients

  • ½ cup Ghee / clarified butter
  • 1 cup Ragi flour
  • 1 cup Jaggery
  • ½ cup Khoya
  • 1 cups Water
  • ½ tsp Cardamom powder
  • Dry fruits for garnish

Methods

Heat ghee in a heavy bottom pan and add Ragi flour on a low to medium flame , roast the flour. Boil water, add grated jaggery till it melt.

Then add the jaggery water to the roasted flour mixture, add grated khoya and mix well. Add cardamom powder and mix well on low flame. Let it cook for a few minutes stirring regularly. Serve hot garnished with dry fruits.
