Cheela with Singhara Atta

Ingredients

  • 3/4 Cup – Desi Atta Company’s Singhara Atta
  • 1 - Green Chilies
  • To Cook - Oil
  • 1/2 tablespoon - Cumin Powder
  • To Taste - Sendha Namak/Rock Salt
  • 1 tablespoon - Chopped Coriander Leaves
  • 1 Cup or needed - Water

Method

- Finely chop green chili, coriander leaves and add in a bowl along Desi Atta Company Singhara atta(Water chestnut Flour), cumin powder, sendha namak and mix.

- Add water to make thin pouring consistency batter.

- Heat a tawa or pan and pour one ladle of batter, don't have to spread it.

- Drizzle oil over edges and cook until edges becomes brown, flip and cook another side for 1 minute or until cheela gets cooked and brown. Repeat for rest.

- Serve hot.
